Chess Openings and ELOs

Background Information

  • Elo
    • A rating system common to chess where the higher the score the better the player.
    • Every player starts with a score of 1,200 and the highest elo of all time is 2,882.
  • ECO
    • Stands for Encyclopedia of Chess Openings.
    • A system that classifies opening moves.
    • Divided into subcategories A-E.
      • For example, all ECO openings that start with A cover different flank openings.
  • "Mixedness"
    • Here "Mixedness" is defined by using the Gini impurity score.
      • More information on Gini impurity
