Background Information

  • Elo:
    • A rating system common to chess where the higher the score the better the player.
    • Every player starts with a score of 1,200 and the highest elo of all time is 2,882.
  • ECO:
    • A system that classifies opening moves.
      • Divided into subcategories A-E (e.g. all ECO openings that start with "A" are versions of flank openings).
  • Interaction between visualizations using the "Top Players Over Time" year slider:
    • The "Chess Opening Results" can be filtered down to a single year.
    • Th bin in "Diversity of Chess Openings for Top Players" that includes the top player for a given year will be highlighted in red.
  • Our visualizations define diversity using the Gini impurity score.
    • More information on Gini impurity
  • These visualizations come from over 300 thousand games of the best players of all time.
